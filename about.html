About ACE GH

Developing the Next Generation of Business Leaders

Our Vision

To put Guelph-Humber students in the best position to succeed.

Our Mission

To provide value for our members through an exciting, professional, and inclusive community.

Who We Are

ACE Guelph-Humber is one of more than 20 ACE Canada chapters across the country. As part of a national network dedicated to student development, we provide opportunities for members to strengthen their professional skills, build meaningful connections, and gain hands-on experience outside the classroom.

Throughout the year, we host case competition workshops, delegate training sessions, practice case drop-ins, networking events, social activities, and professional development opportunities. Members can connect with alumni, faculty, and industry professionals while developing skills in communication, problem-solving, leadership, and presentation.

Our delegates compete at invitational competitions hosted by universities across Ontario and represent Guelph-Humber at ACE Nationals, Canada's largest undergraduate business conference. These experiences allow students to challenge themselves, collaborate with peers from across the country, and apply classroom learning in competitive environments.

ACE Guelph-Humber was recognized with the Largest Chapter Growth Award in 2025 and was named Chapter of the Year in 2026, reflecting our commitment to creating an engaging and impactful experience for our members.

The ACE Experience

More Than Just Competitions

Training & Workshops

Our training sessions are two-hour bi-weekly workshops focused on case analysis, presentation skills, strategy development, and business problem-solving.

Drop-Ins & Support

Our weekly five-hour drop-ins give delegates a chance to stop by, get one-on-one support from executives, ask questions, work through cases, and connect with other members.

Case Competitions

ACE GH hosts Leaders of Tomorrow, our annual case competition at Guelph-Humber. Delegates also compete at invitationals across Ontario and attend ACE Nationals, Canada’s largest undergraduate business case competition conference.

Networking & Community

We host alumni networking events, skill-building workshops with professionals, and socials such as game nights and Dave & Buster’s outings.

Why Join ACE GH?

What Is In It For You?

ACE GH gives students the opportunity to grow professionally, build confidence, and find a strong sense of community at Guelph-Humber.

Professional Growth

Build transferable skills in communication, leadership, teamwork, problem-solving, and public speaking through hands-on experiences beyond the classroom.

Meaningful Connections

Connect with students, alumni, faculty, and industry professionals through networking events, mentorship opportunities, workshops, and competitions.

A Place to Belong

Become part of a welcoming chapter where students support one another, build friendships, and feel connected to something meaningful at Guelph-Humber.
